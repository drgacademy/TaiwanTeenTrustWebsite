隱私權政策

最後更新：2026 年 7 月 12 日

我們蒐集哪些資料

當您透過本網站「加入我們」表單提出申請時，我們會蒐集：中文與英文姓名、電子郵件、就讀（或畢業）學校、畢業年份，以及您填寫的申請動機與期望。

蒐集目的

這些資料僅用於審閱入社申請、安排面談，以及與您聯繫申請相關事宜。我們不會將您的資料用於行銷，也不會提供、出售或分享給任何第三方。

資料保存與安全

申請資料儲存於 Supabase（資料庫代管服務），僅本組織之管理人員可存取。招募週期結束後，未錄取者之申請資料將於合理期間內刪除。

您的權利

依《個人資料保護法》，您可以隨時要求查詢、更正或刪除您的個人資料。請來信 info@taiwanteentrust.org，我們將盡速處理。

Cookie 與分析

本網站使用瀏覽器的 localStorage 記住您的語言偏好，不使用追蹤性 Cookie。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

What we collect

When you apply through the "Join Us" form on this site, we collect: your Chinese and English name, email address, school, expected graduation year, and the answers you write in the application.

Why we collect it

This information is used solely to review your membership application, arrange an interview, and contact you about your application. We do not use it for marketing, and we never sell or share it with third parties.

Storage & security

Application data is stored with Supabase (a managed database service) and is accessible only to this organization's administrators. Applications from candidates who are not admitted are deleted within a reasonable period after each recruitment cycle.

Your rights

Under Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing info@taiwanteentrust.org.

Cookies & analytics

This site uses your browser's localStorage to remember your language preference. We do not use tracking cookies.
