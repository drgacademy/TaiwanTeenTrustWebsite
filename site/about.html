About Us

我們是改變We are 台灣的青年。youth changing Taiwan.

Taiwan Teen Trust（TTT）成立於2024年，由約20名來自台灣各地及海外就讀的高中生共同創立。我們相信青年的力量不應被低估——每一個行動，不論大小，都能為這片土地帶來真實的改變。

Taiwan Teen Trust (TTT) was founded in 2024 by around 20 high school students from schools across Taiwan and abroad. We believe youth power should never be underestimated — every action, big or small, can bring real change to this island.

透過每年設定的年度主題，我們集結設計、影片、研究、行銷、財務等多元專業的成員，針對台灣最迫切的社會議題，發起具體、可見、有影響力的行動方案。目前組織重點在於動物福利與流浪動物救援。

Through our annual focus theme, we unite members from design, film, research, marketing, finance and more to launch concrete, visible, and impactful initiatives. Our current focus centers on animal welfare and stray animal rescue across Taiwan.

Core Values

我們做事的方式How We Work

01 — 行動力Action

做，而不只是說

Do, not just say

我們把資源和精力放在真實可見的行動上，而非停留於討論和計畫。每個專案都有明確的目標、時程與可量化的成果。

We put resources and energy into real, visible actions — not just discussions and plans. Every project has clear goals, timelines, and measurable outcomes.

02 — 多元包容Diversity

每種才能都有舞台

Every talent has a stage

設計師、工程師、研究員、影像創作者、行銷人——我們相信多元的背景與技能，才能打造出真正有影響力的解決方案。

Designers, engineers, researchers, filmmakers, marketers — we believe diverse backgrounds and skills create truly impactful solutions.

03 — 透明負責Accountability

公開我們的每一步

We open every step

從專案規劃到成果報告，我們對社會大眾保持透明。我們相信信任來自責任感，而責任感來自坦誠分享。

From project planning to outcome reports, we maintain transparency with the public. Trust comes from accountability, and accountability from honest sharing.

04 — 持續學習Learning

從每次失敗中成長

Grow from every failure

我們不害怕犯錯，因為我們把每次挑戰都視為學習的機會。組織的成長，源自於成員的成長。

We're not afraid to fail, because we treat every challenge as a learning opportunity. The organization grows as its members grow.

我們的歷程
Our Journey

從這裡開始的故事The Story That Started Here

2024

Taiwan Teen Trust 正式成立

Taiwan Teen Trust Founded

由約20名來自台灣各校及海外就讀的高中生共同發起，以「青年改變社會」為核心信念，正式成立組織並完成第一屆招募。

Founded by ~20 high school students from schools across Taiwan and abroad, built on the core belief that "youth change society," completing the first recruitment cycle.

2025-2026

第一屆年度主題：流浪動物救援

1st Annual Focus: Stray Animal Rescue

推出全台第一個由青年主導的流浪動物救援資訊平台「浪浪援助地圖（Stray Aid Map）」，整合收容所、動物醫院與餵食站數據，讓資訊不再分散。

Launching Taiwan's first youth-led stray animal rescue platform "Stray Aid Map," integrating data from shelters, veterinary clinics, and feeding stations so no animal goes unseen.
